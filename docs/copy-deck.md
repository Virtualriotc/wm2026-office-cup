# Copy Deck — WM 2026 Office Cup

The single source of truth for every word in the product. Written to four standards: **purposeful, concise, conversational, clear** (sentence case, active-imperative buttons, front-loaded, no blame, no jargon, accessible). Legal/disclaimer strings keep the compliance substance from `goal.md` — the voiced versions below still need a German translation + lawyer check before launch.

---

## Voice chart

**Who's talking:** the witty colleague who runs the office pool. Confident, warm, a little cheeky — never corporate, never childish, never trying too hard.

| Concept | Sounds like | Do | Don't |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Matchday banter** | Playful, social, a bit of needle | "Talk smack. Then back it up." | "Engage with your colleagues." |
| **Everyone's welcome** | Inclusive, no football gatekeeping | "Don't know a libero from a latte? Perfect." | Stats-flexing, xG, jargon. |
| **Quietly premium** | Short, sharp, sure of itself | "Two taps. You're in." | "AMAZING!!! 🎉🔥 Sign up NOW!!!" |
| **Respectfully honest** | Clear about the boring-but-important bits, in plain words | "Just for fun. Not an Enpal thing." | Walls of legalese. |

**Rules of thumb:** buttons = 2–4 words, a verb leads. Titles = 3–6 words. Never blame the user. One exclamation mark per screen, max. "You", not "users". Say it out loud — if you wouldn't, rewrite it.

## Notifications (only two — by design)

- **Pre-lock nudge** *(action-required)* — Title: `Picks close in 3 hours` · Body: `You haven't called Matchday 5 yet. Two taps and you're set.`
- **Round recap** *(passive)* — Title: `Matchday 4 is scored` · Body: `You went 3 of 4 and climbed to #12. See the new table.`

## Errors & edge cases

- **Generic system error:** `That didn't go through. Give it another tap in a moment.`
- **Name already taken:** `That name's taken. Try another?`
- **Invalid / expired link:** `This link's expired. Ask the organiser for a fresh one.`
- **Picks after lock (server-rejected):** `This match is locked — kickoff's been and gone. Your earlier pick stands.`

### Before → after (why the old copy was weak)

| Old | New | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| "Pick winners. Drag your department up the table. Two minutes a round." | "Call every winner. Carry your department up the table. Two minutes a round." | Sharper verb, warmer ownership ("carry"). |
| "Let's go ⚽️" | "Start picking →" | A vague cheer → a specific action. |
| "picks close soon" | "Picks close in 3 hours" | Vague → concrete, front-loaded. |
| "you got it +1" | "Nailed it · +1" | Robotic → human, still specific. |
| consent legalese | voiced consent, same legal substance | Plain language, same protection. |
